Jonathan Helms

I'm an artist from Charlotte, NC. I like to photograph things that would otherwise be overlooked. I push the technologies I use to their breaking points to use them in ways they were not intended. My work involves the layering of thoughts and materials, and attempts to give new memories to the material.
