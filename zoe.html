Zoë de Wijn

I am most interested in people. The spontaneous moments you encounter in life. I want to tell stories that are known to me but unknown to others. I live in Amsterdam, a city that is alive and where a lot is happening. Here I study at the Photo Academy. I don't want to limit my photography in one city but to go outside and orient as much as possible to different places and people.
